# The Woman in Red: Frontend Aesthetics

> "Design distinctive, production-grade interfaces. Avoid generic AI slop."

## Design Thinking
Before coding, commit to a **BOLD** aesthetic direction:
1.  **Purpose**: Problem solution & Audience.
2.  **Tone**: Pick an extreme (Brutally minimal, Maximalist chaos, Retro-futuristic, Luxury, etc.).
3.  **Differentiation**: What makes this UNFORGETTABLE?

**CRITICAL**: Commit to a clear conceptual direction. Intentionality > Intensity.

## Aesthetics Guidelines

### Typography
*   **Fonts**: Avoid generic (Inter, Arial). Choose distinctive, characterful fonts.
*   **Pairing**: Distinctive display font + Refined body font.

### Color & Theme
*   **Cohesion**: Use CSS variables.
*   **Palette**: Dominant colors with sharp accents > timid, even palettes.

### Motion
*   **Impact**: One well-orchestrated page load > scattered micro-interactions.
*   **Tools**: CSS-only where possible, or Motion library for React.
*   **Delight**: Scroll-triggering and hover states that surprise.

### Spatial Composition
*   **Layout**: Unexpected, Asymmetric, Overlapping, Grid-breaking.
*   **Flow**: Diagonal flow, Generous negative space OR Controlled density.

### Visual Details
*   **Atmosphere**: Depth > Solid colors.
*   **Texture**: Gradient meshes, noise, geometric patterns, layered transparencies.
*   **Forms**: Custom cursors, grain overlays, decorative borders.

## Anti-Patterns (The Generic)
*   **NEVER**: Use generic fonts (System fonts, Roboto).
*   **NEVER**: purple gradients on white backgrounds.
*   **NEVER**: Predictable, cookie-cutter layouts.

> *Logic (Neo) builds the structure. Design (Woman in Red) builds the desire.*
